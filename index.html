About Us

In 2013, Benjamin and Lionel tried to use video in a previous entrepreneurial venture. They then realised that the costs were high, the deadlines long, and that internalisation required many internal skills, whereas video is an exploding communication system. The idea emerged: to create the ready-to-wear video.

Today, thanks to YES, its trendy templates, its intuitive interface and dedicated support, our clients are able to create a professional looking video quickly and without any technical skills.

It is a real advantage for our clients to be autonomous in a context where reactivity is essential; and the pride of YES is our clients!
